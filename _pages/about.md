Welcome
======

We are an environmental engineering research group focusing on **resource recovery** and **circular economy** technologies.

Our current research topics include:
- **Phosphorus recovery** from organic wastes (e.g., livestock digestate)
- **Biochar-based adsorption** and advanced treatment processes
- Sustainable **waste-to-resource** and carbon-neutral environmental solutions
